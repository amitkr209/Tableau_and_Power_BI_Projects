# Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard Project (Tableau Edition)

## Overview
The **Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard Project** aims to provide deep insights into the store’s sales and order performance across India. This interactive Tableau dashboard enables stakeholders to track revenue trends, analyze customer behavior, and evaluate business performance across multiple dimensions.

## Objective
The primary objective is to create an interactive dashboard that helps Vrinda Store analyze:
- Revenue distribution across categories, states, and sales channels.  
- Customer demographics such as gender and age group.  
- Monthly and quarterly order trends, along with delivery performance.  

## Tools Used
- **Tableau Desktop**: For designing and developing interactive dashboards.  
- **Calculated Fields & Tableau Functions**: For custom KPIs and advanced analysis.  
- **Excel**: Used for preliminary data cleaning and formatting.  
- **Git & GitHub**: For version control and project sharing.  

## Project Description
This project focuses on two dashboards:  

1. **Revenue Dashboard**  
   - Highlights revenue performance by category, state, gender, and sales channel.  
   - Tracks key KPIs like total revenue, average order revenue, and monthly revenue trends.  

2. **Orders Dashboard**  
   - Displays order trends by month, category, state, gender, and channel.  
   - Analyzes delivery status, top-performing states, and order distribution by demographics.

## Dashboard Features
- **Revenue Analysis**: Track ₹21M+ revenue with breakdowns by category, channel, and state.  
- **Order Insights**: Over 31K orders analyzed across 1,860 cities.  
- **Demographic Analysis**: Revenue and orders segmented by gender and age groups.  
- **Geographic Insights**: Top states contributing to sales and revenue.  
- **Channel Distribution**: Evaluate performance across platforms like Amazon, Myntra, Flipkart, and Ajio.  
- **Order Status Tracking**: Delivered vs refunded distribution for operational efficiency.  

## Key Insights
1. **Revenue Leaders**: Maharashtra generated the highest revenue (₹2.99M), followed by Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh.  
2. **Top Categories**: “Set” category contributed over **50% of revenue** and **40% of orders**.  
3. **Gender Distribution**: Women contributed the majority of revenue (64%) and orders (69%).  
4. **Channel Performance**: Amazon (35.5%) and Myntra (23.3%) dominated online sales.  
5. **Seasonal Trends**: Revenue peaked in **March**, while orders were highest during **March–April**.  
6. **Order Status**: ~92% of orders were delivered successfully, with only 1.7% refunded.  

## Recommendations
1. **Leverage Women-Centric Marketing**: Since women drive a majority of sales, campaigns can be optimized around women-focused categories.  
2. **Expand Channel Partnerships**: Boost presence on Amazon and Myntra while growing on Ajio and niche platforms.  
3. **Geographic Expansion**: Target underperforming states beyond Maharashtra and Karnataka for higher penetration.  
4. **Inventory Optimization**: Align stock with high-performing categories like “Set” and “Kurta.”  
5. **Customer Retention**: Introduce loyalty programs to sustain repeat orders, especially from women in the adult age group.
